A Legacy of Over 25 Years


For over twenty years, we’ve honoured the essence of tradition, infusing each dish with our passion and authentic flavours since 1997.

What began as a humble endeavour has evolved into a culinary
institution, renowned for our commitment to preserving the essence
of Hyderabadi cuisine. At Pista House, we
don’t just serve food, we curate experiences that linger in the memory long after the last
bite.
Our secret? It lies in the meticulous craftsmanship that goes into every dish, from our world-famous Haleem to the iconic Biryani that melts in your mouth. Each recipe is a testament to generations of culinary expertise, passed down through our family with care and reverence.

The World Famous Haleem

Indulge in a culinary masterpiece that has captured the hearts of
millions worldwide—the world-famous Haleem of Pista House.
Crafted with meticulous attention to detail and generations-old
recipes, our Haleem is a symphony of flavours, blending tender
meat with wholesome grains and aromatic spices.

Originating from the royal kitchens of Hyderabad, our Haleem has
become a symbol of tradition and excellence, celebrated by food
lovers and experts alike. Bearing the prestigious Geographical
Indication (GI) tag, our Haleem stands as a testament to the
unique culinary heritage of Hyderabad.

Slow-cooked to perfection, each spoonful is a journey through the
rich heritage of Hyderabadi cuisine, leaving a lingering taste that
beckons you back for more. Whether you’re a seasoned enthusiast
or a newcomer to the world of Haleem, prepare to be enchanted by
the unparalleled taste and texture of Pista House’s signature dish.

TeamBehindPistaHouse


Mohd Abdul Majeed

Founder

An enterprising businessman with vision, foresight and drive allied with excellent knowledge on food and customer’s tastes, Mr. Majeed a restaurant Industry veteran holds more than 24 years of food service industry experience and has very closely observed the needs of customers. This inspired him to start his own entrepreneurial venture Pista House which is running successfully at multiple locations.


Mohd Abdul Mohsi

Director

Mr. Mohsi has a singular vision of serving delicious food and bring the utmost comfort, satisfaction, quality and price for the customers. He always dreamt of a concept that would elevate the perception of Indian cuisine to be on par with French, Mexican, Italian etc, a dream that will now be a reality with the success of Pista House UAE.


Mohd Mohddis Ali

Ceo

Following in his fathers’ footsteps, Mr. Mohddis role as an Industrialist has been integral to the extraordinary development of Pista House. With an education in business studies, his well rounded approach is a testament to the success of the family run business.
